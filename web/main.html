What is it all about?

Short volume is the number of shares that have been borrowed to open short positions (when you sell one or more shares of a stock that you don‚Äôt own).

TightShorts.ru aggregates data from FINRA and presents it in an easy-to-use way.

Terminology

  1. Volume - number of shares of all executed trades during regular trading hours
  2. Short Volume - number of shares that have been borrowed to open short positions
  3. Short Exempt Volume - number of shares of all executed short sale exempt trades during regular trading hours
  4. Short Volume Ratio - percentage of short positions from the total volume
  5. Short Exempt Volume Ratio - percentage of short short sale exempt trades from the total volume

Disclaimer: The data on the TightShorts is limited to the combined trading volume on the NASDAQ, NYSE and OTC, which was reported to the FINRA trade reporting facility.
